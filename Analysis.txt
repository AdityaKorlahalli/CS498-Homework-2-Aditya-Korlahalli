The latencies between the US and EU differ because of the distance. Requests to the US are faster because it is closer which means that the EU requests would take a bit longer. Additionally, POST requests to /REGISTER are slower than GET requests to /list as writing data is more time consuming than reading data.

The consistency experiment shows that most new users appear quickly on the other region. Sometimes there can be some delays, but Firestore updates across regions automatically.
